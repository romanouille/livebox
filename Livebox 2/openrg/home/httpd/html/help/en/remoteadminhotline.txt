<b>Remote administration:</b><br>
<br>
For troubleshooting, you can give a hotline operator temporary access to your livebox for 15 minutes.<br>
To enable hotline access for 15 minutes, click the <b>Start</b> button. The hotline access settings are displayed on the screen. Do not give them to anyone other than the hotline operator.<br>
You can extend this temporary access by clicking the <b>Extend</b> button.<br>
For disable temporary hotline access, click the <b>Stop</b> button.<br>
