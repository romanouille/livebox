<b>Remote administration:</b><br>
<br>
To access your livebox remotely, from outside your network, check that the <b>Enable remote access</b> option is checked.<br>
You can then choose either permanent or temporary remote access.<br>
This configuration enables you to access your livebox remotely, from outside your network. Enter the port number to open for external access. You must also enter a username and password for accessing the router. You must remember the username and password as you will need to enter them to access your livebox remotely.<br>
