<b>Internet access restriction:</b><br>
<br>
This feature is used to restrict Internet access for a device. <br>
Once you enable this option, you must define access time ranges using check boxes and pull-down lists. To restrict access to the Internet for a particular day of the week, simply check the corresponding box. To configure the time periods for Internet access, use the pull-down lists. The device can then access the Internet during this time range. Outside the time ranges, the device cannot access the Internet.<br>
