<span class="boldorange">Device description:</span> This section enables you to customise the device. You can enter a network name, specify the device type, assign an icon or specify which room of your house the device is in.<br>
<span class="boldorange">Connection:</span>  This field shows how the device is connected to your livebox, the type of connection (Ethernet, WiFi or USB), IP address, MAC address and status (connected or disconnected).<br>
<span class="boldorange">Delete device:</span> This button can be used to delete the device from your network.<br>
