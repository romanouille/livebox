rescue firmware

a firmware upgrade is in progess. During this process, the blue led of your Livebox will blink during 15mn. When it will be finish, your Livebox will reboot automaticaly.


please do not switch off your Livebox or disconnect the ADSL link during the blue led blinking. this upgrade can't be stopped.


after the upgrade, you will use some news Livebox functions


please, do not switch off your Livebox during the upgrade. if you encounter any problems, please contact the technical support.
